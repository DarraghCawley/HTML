Introduction

This is a college project completed as part of an assignment. This project will highlight the popularity of cats, the trends and changes in their health, the process of adoption and services that help cats in unfortunate conditions

Explanation

The Home page primarily serves as a basis for the appearance of the pages. It's mainly present to display the columns. The Statistics page will highlight a list showing a list of the most popular breeds of cat as of 2024. It will also have a table to display the previous years' trends related to cat health. The media page displays a video explaining the adoption process and has a Google Maps embed that highlights one of the nearest relevant locations. The Contact page displays a contact form.
