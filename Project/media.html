A Valuable Agency

In Ireland, agencies such as "Cat's Trust" are very valuable:

  • They educate the public on how to handle stray cats properly.
  • They provide many options for people looking to adopt a cat while also ensuring the environment is suitable for the cat.
  • As it is a registered charity, the volunteers are trustworthy as there is a lack of financial incentive.
